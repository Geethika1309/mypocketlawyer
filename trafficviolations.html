Traffic Violations

Know your rights and learn what steps to take when faced with traffic fines or violations.

üîç What Are Traffic Violations?

Traffic violations are offenses committed by drivers while operating a vehicle. In India, these are governed under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 and include:

  • Driving without a valid license
  • Over-speeding or rash driving
  • Not wearing a helmet or seatbelt
  • Signal jumping and lane violations
  • Driving under the influence (DUI)
  • Using mobile phone while driving

üõ† Steps to Take If You Are Fined

  • Check the challan status on echallan.parivahan.gov.in
  • Pay the fine online using UPI, Netbanking, or card.
  • If wrongly fined, visit your local traffic police station with evidence (CCTV footage, documents, etc.).
  • Keep a digital or printed copy of the payment receipt.

üìÑ Required Documents

  • Driving License
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC)
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Pollution Under Control (PUC) Certificate
  • Challan receipt (if already fined)

üìå Important Tips

  • Always follow speed limits and wear safety gear.
  • Install a dashcam‚Äîit can be useful in case of disputes.
  • Save the helpline numbers of local traffic departments.
