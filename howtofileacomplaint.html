üìÑ How to File a Complaint

üìã Understanding the Complaint Process

Filing a complaint is a legal process that helps you formally raise your grievance to authorities. Complaints can be filed in-person at a police station, consumer forum, or through online government portals depending on the nature of the issue.

üìù Steps to File a Complaint

  • Identify the issue and collect all evidence (photos, receipts, chats, etc.)
  • Write a formal complaint letter explaining the incident clearly.
  • Visit the relevant authority: Police Station, Cyber Cell, or Consumer Court.
  • Submit your complaint with your documents and get an acknowledgment copy.
  • Follow up periodically if no action is taken.

üìÑ Documents You May Need

  • Government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Proof of the incident (screenshots, photos, invoices, etc.)
  • Copy of the complaint letter
  • Supporting witness statements (if any)
