Email Setup Instructions

  1. Create a free account at EmailJS.com
  2. Add Gmail as your email service (pocketlawyerindia1950@gmail.com)
  3. Create an email template for password reset emails
  4. Get your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key from EmailJS dashboard
  5. Enter these details in the form above and click "Save Configuration"

Until configured, notifications will only appear in the browser console.

Not receiving emails?

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Verify you entered the correct email address
  • Ensure your email inbox is not full
  • Allow a few minutes for delivery
