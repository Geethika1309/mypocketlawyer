Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pocket Lawyer completely free? Free Service

Yes! Pocket Lawyer is 100% free to use. We believe that legal assistance should be accessible to everyone regardless of their financial situation. There are no hidden fees, subscription costs, or premium paywalls - all features are completely free.

How is Pocket Lawyer free? What's the catch?

There's no catch! Pocket Lawyer was created by students as an educational project to help make legal knowledge accessible to all Indians. We're funded through our educational institution and believe in the mission of democratizing legal information. We may explore non-intrusive sponsorship options in the future, but the core services will always remain free.

Is Pocket Lawyer a substitute for a real lawyer?

Pocket Lawyer is designed to provide legal information and document generation services, but it does not replace personalized legal advice from a qualified attorney for complex matters. We recommend consulting with a lawyer for serious legal issues. Think of us as a first step in understanding your legal situation.

How accurate is the legal information provided?

We strive to provide accurate and up-to-date legal information based on Indian law. Our content is regularly reviewed, but laws can change, so we always recommend verifying critical information. For the most current legal advice, consult with a practicing lawyer.

Are the documents generated legally valid?

Yes, the documents generated through our platform are created using standard legal templates and are legally valid when properly executed. However, specific requirements may vary by situation and jurisdiction. We recommend having important documents reviewed by a legal professional if possible.

Who can use Pocket Lawyer?

Pocket Lawyer is available to all Indian citizens and residents. Our services are designed for individuals who need basic legal information and document generation. Whether you're a student, professional, business owner, or just someone with legal questions, our platform is here to help.

How often is the legal information updated?

We strive to update our legal information regularly, especially when there are changes in Indian law. However, the legal landscape can change quickly, so we recommend cross-checking critical information with official government sources or legal professionals.

Can I request specific legal documents or information?

Yes! We're always looking to expand our services based on user needs. You can contact us with suggestions for new document templates or legal information you'd like to see added to our platform. We'll do our best to accommodate popular requests.

How do I get the most out of Pocket Lawyer?

To get the most from our platform: 1) Explore all our law categories to understand what we offer, 2) Use the document generation feature for any legal paperwork needs, 3) Submit questions through our "Ask a Lawyer" feature, and 4) Check back regularly as we're constantly adding new content and features.
